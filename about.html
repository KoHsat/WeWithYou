About Us

Why We Started

We saw students being left behind by problems they did not create.

Many students miss lectures, struggle to submit assignments, or fall behind simply because they do not have stable internet access or enough digital tools at home. We With You was created to respond to that gap with practical, community-based support.

Our Purpose

We support students and low-income families who experience limited or unstable internet access, making sure they can continue learning with greater confidence and dignity.

Our Mission

To bridge the digital divide through access, support, and shared opportunity.

We believe education should not depend on income level, location, or device ownership. Our work is about making digital learning more reachable for every student.

The Problem

Students without stable internet often lose access to online classes, digital assignments, and important educational tools that others take for granted.

Our Response

Instead of waiting for learners to come to us, we bring the learning space to them through the Mobile Hub, offline materials, and community-driven support.
