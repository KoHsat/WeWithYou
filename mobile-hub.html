Mobile Hub

A community learning room inside a van.

Built to reach students where they are.

The Mobile Hub is a small learning room in a van that brings digital support directly into neighborhoods. It offers internet access, shared devices, and offline learning tools for students who need flexible support.

Inside The Van

Everything is designed to help students keep learning.

What’s Inside

  • WiFi access for study sessions and class tasks
  • Several shared computers for digital schoolwork
  • USB-based offline learning kits
  • A safe and focused community learning area

Who Can Join

Students and low-income families who need better access to internet, devices, and learning materials are welcome to use the Mobile Hub and its support resources.
